We Are Cove Markets

We built Cove Markets in order to solve the problems that we ourselves faced as investors. Our founders each bring more than a decade of experience from top-tier technology-driven trading firms and software providers. Cove Markets will offer services to allow professional traders and institutions to achieve best execution for a wide range of digital assets in a fully transparent, easy-to-use, algorithmic, and secure fashion. You deserve confidence that you are always getting the best price in the market with just a click.

Our Team

Scott Knudsen

Scott is CEO of Cove Markets. He spent 14 years at a top proprietary trading firm, working in Chicago, Amsterdam, and Hong Kong and has served on the Advisory Boards of Cboe Global Markets and Northwestern University's Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department. He holds a BS in Electrical Engineering from Northwestern University.

Victor Glava

Victor is CTO of Cove Markets. Prior to Cove, Victor co-founded and served as CTO of a Chicago-based financial technology firm for professional traders and risk managers that ultimately became a global leader in electronic trading solutions. He holds a BS in computer engineering and an MS in computer science from the Illinois Institute of Technology.

Michael Kogan

Michael is a board member of Cove Markets. He has had a successful career as a quantitative trader and angel investor. Michael has also invested in companies such as Robinhood, Influxdata, Endaga and MotionAI. He holds an MBA in Analytic Finance from University of Chicago.
